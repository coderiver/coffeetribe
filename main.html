Somewhere in the middle of the tropical island of Phuket there lives a tribe of coffee lovers. They worship great tasting coffee, they crave fresh and healthy food, they seek vitamin stuffed freshly squeezed juices, they indulge on mouth watering deserts. They are called Coffee Tribe.


Come join us at a place where you can stay as long as you want to, relax in lovely comfortable chairs, listen to some laid back music, have a cup of fantastic coffee, strike up a casual conversation with a friendly stranger next to you or just enjoy your fresh juice in total peace and quiet. You can forget about time here, at least for a while.
Coffee Tribe is a worry-free zone.
